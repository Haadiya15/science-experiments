Rock Candy Experiment

To grow sugar crystals and observe the process of crystallization.!

Materials:

  • - 2 cups of water
  • - 4 cups of granulated sugar
  • - A clean jar
  • - A wooden skewer or string
  • A clothespin or tape
  • Food coloring (optional)

Steps:

  1. 1. Boil the water in a saucepan, then gradually add the sugar, stirring continuously until dissolved. Add more sugar until no more can dissolve (about 4 cups).
  2. 2. Allow the sugar solution to cool slightly.
  3. 3. Pour the solution into the jar.
  4. 4. Attach the skewer or string to a clothespin or tape so that it hangs in the center of the jar, not touching the sides or bottom.
  5. 5. Optionally, add food coloring to the jar.
  6. 6. Let the jar sit undisturbed in a cool, dry place for 3-7 days.
  7. 7. Crystals will begin to form around the skewer or string.
